A public archive for Florida wetland maps, development pressure, and regional hydrologic context.

The Blackwater Vault is a map containment area: one public place for final map exports, supporting methodology, workflow notes, evidence documents, and plain-language interpretation. It starts with one strong map and grows by region.

Open the map archive Read the method

What this is

A public-facing GIS archive built to show how Florida development pressure interacts with wetlands, waterbodies, protected lands, and likely surface-flow behavior. The maps are regional first, using Water Management Districts as the main frame.

What this is not

This is not a legal wetland delineation, engineering flood model, permit decision, or parcel-level hydraulic study. It is a reproducible map system for seeing spatial relationships and identifying places that deserve closer review.
