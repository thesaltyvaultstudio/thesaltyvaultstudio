Method

Built for reproducibility.

The Vault separates source data, regional analysis, field review, display cleanup, and public interpretation.

Core rules

Preserve originals

Source datasets stay intact. Processing happens on working copies.

Work by district

Water Management Districts are the regional frame for map production.

Keep regional outputs locked

Validated regional outputs are not edited to force a local result.

Use AOIs as close-up views

Areas of Interest refine local review without rewriting the regional model.

Hydrologic interpretation

Flow accumulation is used to show likely flow preference and regional pressure. It does not claim exact flood depth or formal engineering results.

Plain-language rule: regional maps discover patterns. local review tests claims.

Display cleanup

Waterbody masks may hide visual artifacts inside lakes. This is a cartographic correction, not a data edit.
